<p><strong>TABLE TYPE DRILLING MACHINES</strong><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">These series of table type drilling machines automate your plate drilling operations for higher throughput and precise hole positions. Plates can be stacked up to the maximum thickness capacity and drilled together for even higher productivity. Simple to operate, reliable and easy to maintain. The normal speed models operate with hydraulic motors up to a maximum of 600 rpm with hole drilling diameter from 32mm to 50mm and maximum thickness of 100mm. The high speed models operate up to a maximum of 3000 rpm with hole diameter capacity of 50mm as standard and 100mm as option, and standard drilling thickness of 100mm. </span><p> <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Sizes range from 1m x 2m to 2m x 4m for the normal speed models and up to 8m x 8m for the high speed models.
