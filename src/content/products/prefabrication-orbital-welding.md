<p><b>Prefabrication Orbital Welding</b><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Orbital welding involves rotating a Tungsten electrode around a tube to weld it to other tubes or accessories.</span><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our programme incorporates a unique and atypical orbital welding machine for so-called “prefab” because it cannot be brought to the tube, it is the tube that is brought to the machine. </span><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">It is an axial loading machine which involves being able to pull out the tube as well as the welded accessory. </span><p><span style="font-weight: 400;">These machines that use standard cutting and bevelling frames have the advantage of being very robust and easy to use. In fact, the SX range is mainly intended for workshop or building site prefabrication use.
