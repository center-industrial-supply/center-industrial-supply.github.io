Product description

PLA-CUT 101D

The PLA-CUT 101D is quite compact & energy efficient and find applications in small workshop, large factories or even in open field site. Comes standard with manual cutting torch. 

  • Dual tip design: Thin plates can be cut with the single tip for smaller kerf and thick plates can be cut with dual tips for a better cut quality.
  • Higher cutting speeds: Compared to conventional type, dual tip design increases the cutting speed.
  • When cutting thick plate, inner tip is completely protected and tip life is also increased.
  • Highly energy efficient IGBT digital inverters saves up to 40 % power and less noise as compared to conventional plasma.
  • Easier operation: No stand-off is required, making operation easier.

Duty Cycle at  40oC (up to 1hr) 100 %

Input Cutting Gases Compressed Air at 5.5 ~ 6.0 Bar

Torch Cooling Method Forced Air cooled

Maximum Output Current Amp 100

Input Power KVA 18

Torch Cooling Method Forced Air cooled

Maximum Cutting thicknes mm 20

Severance Cut mm 40

Dimensions (LxWxH) mm 510 x 360 x 670

Weight Kgs 62
